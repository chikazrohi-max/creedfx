Strategy 1 — Liquidity Sweep Reversal

A precision-based Gold strategy designed to exploit stop-hunts at key session highs and lows.

Market Conditions

  • Instrument: Gold (XAUUSD)
  • Sessions: London & New York only
  • Frequency: 1–2 trades per session max
  • Best Days: High-impact news or expansion days

Execution Rules

  • Price sweeps previous session high or low
  • Rejection candle closes back inside range
  • Entry on confirmation, not impulse
  • Stop-loss beyond sweep extreme

Visual Execution Model

Price sweeps liquidity at key session highs or lows, then aggressively rejects.

CREEDFX enters only after the market proves intent.

Risk Management

  • Risk per trade: 0.25%–0.5%
  • TP1: 2.5R
  • TP2: 4R
  • Partial profits mandatory
